    • FA Questions

    + What is WEETNET?
    WEETNET is a spreadsheet providing links to products from MuleBuy, such as those on Taobao and Weidian. Its goal is to inform buyers, simplify the purchasing process, and prevent fraud. WEETNET does not endorse counterfeit goods or sell any physical products.
    + Is using WEETNET easy?
    Yes, WEETNET is designed to be user-friendly. It offers a clear interface for searching products and accessing verified seller links.
    + Can I use WEETNET on mobile devices?
    Yes, the WEETNET website is fully optimized for mobile devices, making it convenient to use on smartphones and tablets.
    + Can I purchase products without an agent?
    Directly buying from Chinese platforms like Taobao and Weidian can be challenging due to language barriers and payment methods. We recommend using a purchasing agent like MuleBuy to ensure a smooth ordering and shipping process.
    + What are the advantages of using WEETNET?
    WEETNET provides verified product links, reducing the risk of fraud. Additionally, it offers a community and support via Discord, where you can share experiences and get advice.
    + How can I join the WEETNET community?
    You can join our Discord server, which is available 24/7. There, you can get support and connect with other community members.
